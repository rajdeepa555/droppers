Product description

Size:Pack of 1  |  Style:32 oz.

Prestone Power Steering Fluid with Stop Leak serves as an excellent preventative maintenance product to help avoid future leaks. Formulated with high-quality oil and fortified with additives to revitalize dried, shrunken, and hardened seals, this power steering fluid helps stop or reduce fluid loss caused by leaky seals. Compatible with factory fluids, it meets service requirements of GM, Ford, Chrysler and many global manufacturers. This formula contains several key ingredients to ensure smooth system operation: anti-wear agents to protect pump components; antioxidants to prevent sludge formation; seal swell agent to protect and rejuvenate seals; antifoam agents to prevent pump cavitation and steering loss; corrosion inhibitors to protect metal components; and pour point depressants for good steering even in low temperatures. Keeps power-steering systems running smoothly, and mixes with all approved power-steering fluids.

Size: Pack of 1|Style: 32 oz.|Verified Purchase
I’ve had a slow leak in my power steering for several years now. Fortunately, it only leaks when the car is parked and off, but up until recently I’ve just been using cheap power steering fluid from the dollar store to top it off every couple days.

This stuff is thicker and clearly better, but it has only slowed my leak instead of stopping it. It definitely is superior quality, though, because when I used the cheapo stuff my steering would groan when it got towards the minimum fill level, but doesn’t complain at all when it’s at the same level with this stuff.

For the equivalent price, I would get almost twice as much cheapo PS fluid, but since this stuff slows the leak down, I think it’s made things roughly equivalent.
